AI/ML Department

The AI/ML department is dedicated to exploring and advancing the cutting-edge fields of AI and machine learning, with the aim of developing intelligent systems and algorithms that can tackle some of the world's most pressing challenges.


The department offers a range of undergraduate and graduate courses in AI and machine learning, providing students with a solid foundation in the theory and practical applications of these fields. The faculty are leading experts in AI and machine learning, with extensive research and industry experience, and are dedicated to mentoring and guiding students towards success. The courses are designed to challenge students to think creatively and to develop innovative solutions to complex problems.


In addition to the academic programs, the department is actively engaged in research in various areas of AI and machine learning, such as natural language processing, computer vision, and deep learning. The research projects are aimed at developing innovative solutions to some of the most challenging problems facing our world, such as healthcare, environmental sustainability, and cybersecurity.


The department also maintains strong ties with industry partners, collaborating with leading companies to develop practical applications of AI and machine learning. Through these partnerships, our students have the opportunity to work on real-world projects, gaining valuable hands-on experience and building industry connections.


The department is committed to advancing the frontiers of AI and machine learning, and to preparing the next generation of leaders in this exciting and rapidly evolving field.
